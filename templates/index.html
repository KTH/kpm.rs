KTH Personal Menu

A development project trying to improve your access to all of KTH

The personal menu has been around at KTH for a long time. Now is the time to make a new start and try to make it better.

Currently, this new start is pretty much a blank canvas. It is up to us in the kpm development team to make it work, but it is up to you, the student, faculty, or staff at KTH to tell it what it should contain.

What new features should we try to include? What parts of the old personal menu is better left out, and what parts are important to include in the new menu?
